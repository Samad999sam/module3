Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish. It consists of tender chicken pieces marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce with butter, cream, and various spices such as garam masala, ginger, garlic, and fenugreek leaves. It is often served with naan bread or rice..

Couscous

Couscous is a traditional North African dish made from tiny steamed balls of semolina. It is a staple food in many countries in the Maghreb region, such as Morocco, Algeria, and Tunisia. Couscous can be served as a side dish or as a main course with vegetables, meat, or fish. It is light, fluffy, and has a slightly nutty flavor.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern street food consisting of thinly sliced meat (commonly chicken, beef, lamb, or a combination thereof) that is stacked in a cone-like shape on a vertical rotisserie. The meat is seasoned with various spices and slowly roasted as it rotates on the spit. Once cooked, the tender and flavorful meat slices are typically served in a pita bread or flatbread wrap along with vegetables, tahini sauce, hummus, and pickles. Shawarma is known for its rich, savory taste and is enjoyed worldwide.
